Cheeseburger

You can never go wrong with a good old american cheeseburger! It never gets old, I may go a long time without eating one but when I get that urge to dig into one it always hits the spot. I enjoy my burgers with almost everything it comes with, pickles, tomatoes, onions, lettuce. It all adds to the burger in the right ways, perfection!

Ingredients for a Cheeseburger

  • 80/20 Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Hamburger Buns
  • Medium Cheddar or American Cheese Slices
  • Thinly Sliced Onion
  • Green Leaf Lettuce
  • Ripe Tomato Slices

How to Prepare a CheeseBurger the Pablo Way

  1. Clean your grill with a scraper, then wipe it down.
  2. Turn on the gril and let it get hot
  3. Season your patty with the salt and pepper
  4. Place the patty on the grill and cook untill the juices are coming out to the top of the patty
  5. Flip the Patty using a spatula
  6. Place a slice of cheeseon the patty and let it cook for another 30 seconds or until ready
  7. Take the patty off the grill and onto the bottom half of a hamburger bun
  8. Assemble your burger however you'd like and enjoy!
