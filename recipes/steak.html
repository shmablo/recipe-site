Steak

One of my favorite things to eat for dinner is steak. However, I like my steak cooked in a very particular way. I like to cook my steak to a nice medium well with a good amount of red in the middle. I think medium well is perfect, it doesn't feel under cooked but you still get the juices of the steak.

How to Grill a Steak the Pablo Way

  • Clean your grill with a scraper, then wipe it down.
  • Turn on the gril and let it get hot
  • While it's getting hot start cutting your steak into slices and drizzle salt on them
  • When ready, put them on the grill and let it cook until you see the juices start coming out the top
  • Flip the steak and let it cook for about 30 seconds
  • Take steak off with a tong and place on cutting board
  • Cut into pieces and enjoy!

Some Sides I like to Enjoy My Steak With

  1. French Fries
  2. Tostones
  3. Mashed Potatoes
  4. Steak Sauce
